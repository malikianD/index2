Welcome to some of the World's Healthiest Food

Today we will be expressing our interest in two dishes that have made it on our list of some of the world's healthiest, (if not tastiest), foods in the world. Our two dishes are Greek Garbanzo Bean Salad and the world famous Lemon Meringue Pie. In the following pages you will be introduced to these two recipes so you may have the opportunity to try these for yourself and discover the true bliss of these dishes.

Greek Garbonzo Bean Salad

Rich in protein and dietary fiber, this fresh-tasting salad provides 11% of your Daily Value (DV) for protein and 26% DV for dietary fiber. It only takes minutes to prepare and gets tastier the longer it marinates. Make this quick and easy recipe a regular addition to your Healthiest Way of Eating.

Lemon Meringue Pie

Lemon meringue pie is a type of baked pie, usually served for dessert, made with a crust usually made of shortcrust pastry, lemon custard filling and a fluffy meringue topping. Lemon meringue pie is prepared with a bottom pie crust, with the meringue directly on top of the lemon filling.
